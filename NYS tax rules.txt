

=== New York Tax Rules

NYS = New York State

workdays are only Monday through Friday. Weekends, vacation, sick day, are not workdays

Two kinds of work days

1. NYS workday include
	A. Work in NYS regardless of domicile location
	XXX do not add Convenience of the Employer (COE) rule here. NYC tax form count these (WFH while work for NYS employer) separately and add to NYS workday to calculate COE

2. non-NYS workday include
	X. Work days outside NYS
	Y. Work days during work travel to other states (not NYS)

=== NYS non-Resident Tax Form IT-203F Sched B rules

Don't count vacation and sick day on weekends and holidays
If vacation day, don't count as sick day
Only count work from home day if it is a workday
