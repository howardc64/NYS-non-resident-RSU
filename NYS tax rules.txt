

=== New York Tax Rules

NYS = New York State

NYS Tax Rules

work days are only Monday through Friday. Weekends are not work days

Two kinds of work days

1. NYS work days include
	A. Work in NY regardless of domicile location
	B. Work from home days when working for NY company

2. non-NYS work days include
	X. Work days outside NYS counts as non NYS-work days. Except 1B
	Y. Work days during work travel to other states (not NYS)
